Lasagna


Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta and also the name of a classic Italian dish made by layering flat, wide pasta sheets with various fillings, typically including ragù (meat and tomato sauce), béchamel, and cheese.It's known for its rich flavors and satisfying texture, often baked in the oven until bubbly and golden brown.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Eggs
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
